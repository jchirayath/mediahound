How to use MediaHound

A quick tour of what you can do here. Unlock 🔒 Admin to edit your catalog and see the editing tools.

🔎 Browsing & finding things
  • Search matches title, artist, genre, cast, studio/label, even track names.
  • Sort by title, year, recently-added, estimated value, rating — or My rating in admin.
  • Filters: format, genre, studio/label, streaming service, language, category, seen/unseen. Clear resets them.
  • 🎬 Movies / 🎵 Music / 📚 Books / 🎮 Games / 🎧 Audiobooks tabs appear once your catalog has more than one type; they narrow the filters to that type.
  • Searching a song surfaces the album it's on — the matching track is highlighted in the album's tracklist.
  • 🎲 Surprise me picks something from whatever's currently filtered (preferring unseen).
  • Per row (− / +) changes how many cards show per row; it's remembered on this device.
🖼️ On each card
  • Click the cover to zoom; use ‹ › to flip through multiple photos of a title.
  • ▶ / ♫ / 📖 / 🎮 / 🎧 pills link to where to watch, listen, find, play or hear it; the resale figure links to live eBay sold listings (and 📊 PriceCharting for games).
  • Music cards expand to a tracklist (♫ N tracks); click a genre, person, studio, or narrator to filter the grid to it.

🔓 Admin tools (you're signed in) — every edit saves to data/ and survives the next rebuild.

✎ Editing your catalog
  • Click a title (or ✎ Edit) to fix name, year, format, and the type-specific fields (artist / author / narrator / developer, studio / label / publisher), or move a title between any media type. Tick re-query to re-fetch metadata online on the next rebuild.
  • Mark as seen, ⟳ Rotate, set a photo as the default (★), delete a photo (🗑), or delete the title.
  • ↻ Rebuild re-bakes the catalog from your edits and reloads.
➕ Adding things
  • ➕ Add by photo — snap or drag in a photo of the cover, or the barcode (UPC/EAN/ISBN) for an exact-release match — decoded on the server, so it works from any phone over Wi-Fi. On a phone, choose Take Photo.
  • 📷 Scan barcode — type the UPC/EAN/ISBN to identify the exact release. (A live in-browser camera scanner also exists, but it needs HTTPS and isn't supported on iOS — photographing the barcode above is the portable way.)
  • ⬆ Import list — paste or upload a CSV of titles (optionally enrich online for art + details).
  • 💿 Discogs — import an entire Discogs collection by username.
⭐ Your personal catalog private
  • Give any item a ★ rating (1–10), a note, and shelves/tags; filter by shelf.
  • 📤 Loan out tracks who has what — a badge shows on-loan items, with an On loan / Available filter.
  • All of this is admin-only and never published — it's stripped from any public site you publish.
💾 Backup, share & libraries
  • ⤓ Export → a 🖨 printable inventory (PDF) (grouped + valued, great for insurance), CSV, JSON, or 🎬 Letterboxd; ⬇ Backup downloads a zip of your whole library.
  • 🌐 Publish deploys a public, read-only copy of your catalog to the web (Netlify).
  • 📚 Library opens, creates, or switches between catalogs (e.g. movies vs. music vs. games) without restarting.
  • ⚙ Settings sets the library name, logo, which fields show, columns, the admin password, and provider API keys.
ℹ️ Good to know
  • The default view is public & read-only; editing needs the 🔒 Admin password.
  • Online lookups (cover art, where-to-watch, barcodes) only happen when you ask — a plain rebuild is offline.
  • More: github.com/jchirayath/mediahound
